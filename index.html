Minesweeper

Welcome to Minesweeper! Here's a quick introduction:

  1. Left click opens a tile.
  2. Right click flags the tile as an assumed bomb.
  3. Click the smiley face (validate button) once you've opened all the tiles that aren't bombs. Make sure you've opened all tiles otherwise you lose!
  4. Cheat on the cheat button to show where the bombs are. Click on it a second time to hide the bombs again.

You can show this dialog again by clicking on the help button at any time.
